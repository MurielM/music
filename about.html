Muriel MacDonald is a singer-songwriter, writer and activist based in San Francisco, CA. Originally from Arlington, VA, Muriel writes a mix of comedy songs and more serious stuff inspired by folk tunes and jazz harmonies. Big influences include Joni Mitchell, Regina Spektor and Tom Lehrer.

To keep up to date with Muriel's music, you can subscribe to her mailing list for very infrequent but totally awesome updates.
